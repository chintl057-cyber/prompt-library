# 📂 04 — Reporting Workflow

**Business function:** Retail Operations — Performance & Strategy

**Trigger:** Completion of inventory and risk analysis workflows

**Prompts in this section:** P09, P10

---

## Section Purpose

These prompts generate structured reports and strategic insights for management decision-making. Retail reporting is often manual and inconsistent. This workflow standardises reporting, improves clarity, and supports faster, data-driven strategic decisions.
